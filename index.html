Study tips:

Read ahead of lectures

Reading ahead of lectures allows you to not only participate but retain the information you have learned!

Go to bed early

Going to bed early makes sure you are refreshed for the lecture. This means you are more alert and retain more information.

Take written notes

Written notes are scientifically proven to help with comprehension
